A Celestial Atlas of Almost-Meetings

Constellations of Longing

A reflective take on New York City's missed connections, this project turns each fleeting message into a star in the night sky.

Each phrase appears as a distinct fragment from someone's search for connection, visible for a few moments before fading away.

Hover over any phrase to bring up its complete story—the detail panel stays open until you click the × to close it.

At any time, just 25 stories are visible, each lingering for a moment before fading and is replaced by another whisper.
