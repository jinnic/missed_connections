Missed Connections Galaxy

This contemplative interpretation of New York City's missed connections presents fleeting moments as gentle stars in the night sky.

Each phrase appears as a distinct fragment from someone's search for connection, visible for a few moments before fading away.

Move your cursor near any phrase to reveal the complete story. The story panel will stay open until you close it using the × button.

Only 15 stories appear at once, each lingering for a moment before fading away, occasionally replaced by another whisper from the vast collection of human encounters.
